About SciTechnia

SciTechnia is an education program inspired by research‑based physics competitions where students master STEM skills in a short time through experiential learning. We provide training and facility support for students interested in this hand‑on learning style and give back to the competitions that inspired us.

Programs

We offer a range of programs to support our students in their STEM journey

Experimental Physics Workshops

Interested in physics experiments? Or thinking about participating in CaYPT or IYPT? Boost your experimental physics knowledge by attending our experimental physics workshops. Topics include classical mechanics, E&M, thermal+optics. Choose the right one for you.

STEM Skills Workshops

Thinking about a career in engineering? Want to build a personal engineering project to test your skills? Join us at our STEM skills workshops. Topics include basic electronics, working with microcontrollers, CAD modeling+3D printing and much more!

Lab Space and Maker Space Access

We provide students with access to part of our facility when we are not hosting workshops. It’s a perfect place for you to complete a small research project with your team or to prepare for a competition like CaYPT.
