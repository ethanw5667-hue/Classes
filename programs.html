Core Physics Pathway (CPP)

A three‑level sequence pairing lectures with hands‑on labs. Each level runs ~8 weeks with weekly 2‑hr theory + alternating 2‑hr lab blocks.

CPP‑1

Foundations: Motion, Forces & Measurement

Prereq: Grade 9 Math. No prior physics required.

  • Kinematics in 1D/2D; uncertainty & error bars
  • Forces, energy, momentum; modeling real data
  • Labs: ticker‑tape & video tracker, Atwood machine, energy loss on ramps
  • Tools: Vernier/DIY sensors, spreadsheet analysis, Python optional

Outcome: Write a lab report with proper uncertainty and claim‑evidence‑reasoning.

CPP‑2

Systems: Oscillations, Fluids & Thermo

Prereq: CPP‑1 or Grade 11 Physics equivalent.

  • Simple & damped harmonic motion; resonance
  • Fluids: Bernoulli, viscosity, buoyancy experiments
  • Thermodynamics: calorimetry, cooling curves
  • Labs: mass‑spring Q‑factor, Stokes’ law viscometer, Newton’s law of cooling

Outcome: Fit models to data and evaluate goodness‑of‑fit.

CPP‑3

Fields: E&M, Waves/Optics & Intro Modern

Prereq: CPP‑2 or Grade 12 Physics equivalent.

  • Circuits, magnetic forces, induction demos
  • Optics: lenses, diffraction, interferometry basics
  • Intro modern: photoelectric effect, spectra
  • Labs: RC time constant, Helmholtz coil mapping, single/double‑slit analysis

Outcome: Design and present a mini‑investigation with a defended conclusion.
